# Brand

**Tim Raysby — the operator.**

## Slogan
MY TOKENS. MY BUSINESS.

## Subhead
I build local-first AI systems that do not rent your data to anyone.

## Thesis
Most of the AI economy is a tax on attention. The cloud-AI mainstream wants every prompt, every memory, and every artifact you produce to live on someone else's hardware, billed by the token. There is a different way: infrastructure that runs on the machine in front of you. Sovereign tokens. Sovereign business. Hence the slogan.
