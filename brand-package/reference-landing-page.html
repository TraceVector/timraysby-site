Questions

Straight answers.
No runaround.

Do you work with clients outside of South Carolina?

+
Yes. Everything is delivered remotely via call, email, and file handoff. I've based myself in Simpsonville, SC, but the work itself is location-agnostic. Discovery calls happen over Zoom or Google Meet at a time that works for your zone.

Who actually does the work — you or an AI?

+
Both. AI handles the heavy lifting on iteration, research, and first-draft generation. I handle the taste, the strategic calls, and everything that needs human judgment — which is still most of what makes a brand memorable. If you wanted pure AI output, you'd just run the prompts yourself.

What if I already have a brand I want to refine?

+
Start with The Spark. I'll audit what you have, tell you what's working, and scope a targeted refresh rather than a full rebuild. Most brands don't need a teardown — they need focus.

Is the $350 USPTO filing fee included in The Forge?

+
No — you pay the filing fee directly to USPTO through your own account. This is intentional: trademarks belong to you, not to me, and the ownership chain stays clean. I handle the research, application drafting, and submission. You pay the government.

What if I hate the first concept?

+
That's what Phase 2 is for. The Forge includes one round of major direction revision and two rounds of refinement on the chosen direction. If we can't align on direction after Phase 2, you keep all deliverables to that point and we part ways cleanly.

Do you work evenings or weekends?

+
Yes — this is a side practice. I have a full-time role during business hours. Calls happen evenings (7–9pm ET) or weekends by mutual agreement. Delivery timelines reflect this pace.

Ready to ship?

Most engagements start with a 20-minute no-pitch call. Tell me about your business, your timeline, and what "done" looks like to you. I'll tell you if I can help.
